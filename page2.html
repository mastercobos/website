Improvement of a Heat Pump Monitoring and Data Acquisition System

In this section, I'll share some details about my thesis. The thesis was done in a laboratory and involved the modification of a comercial aerothermal heat pump. The project can be divided in:

  • Improvement of the sensor system in an aerothermal heat pump.
  • Implementation of analogical and Modbus RTU sensors on the Siemens PLC S7-1500.
  • Development on Tia Portal v17 of the data acquisition and monitoring system.

In the accompanying video, you'll observe a demonstration of the monitoring system (Siemens HMI screen). The menus allow monitoring various parameters, tracking the temperature and pressure of the refrigerant throughout the thermodynamic cycle, observing the reservoir temperature, and assessing the energy consumed by the heat pump and compressor. Additionally, it is possible to focus on the evolution of a variable by clicking on it.

Accessing the PLC web server allows you to download a CSV file containing the data stored at the selected rate.
