The objective of this website is to provide a brief introduction to my academic life and job experience. Additionally, I intend to explain some of the projects I have undertaken in the past.

In 2017, I commenced my studies in Energy Engineering at University of Malaga. Over time, I realized my desire to pursue international experiences.

In 2021, I relocated to Seville to further my studies in University of Seville. While there, I pursued a specialization within my degree titled Power Production Systems. The specialization focused on conventional energy generation systems, including Combined Cycle Power Plants, Nuclear Power Plants, etc.

In 2022, I continued my studies in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil. From August onward, I spent 5 months in Niteroi, attending Fluminense Federal University.

Itaipú hydroelectric dam, (Foz do Iguaçu, Brazil).

By January 2023, I had returned from Brazil and began a trainee job. The role centered around Renewable Energies projects, with a major focus on designing photovoltaic systems for self-consumption. The internship concluded at the end of June.

At that time, all that remained for me to graduate was completing my thesis. Come September, I successfully presented my thesis to the University. The project received a grade of 9.5 out of 10 and went on to become a finalist for an energy efficiency award.
